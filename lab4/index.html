Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

Making Guac

  1. Cut open avocados
  2. Remove pit and take out flesh
  3. Mash flesh in bowl
  4. Cut open a lemon
  5. Squeeze lemon juice on mash and mix in
  6. Dice up a Tomato
  7. Add tomato to mash
  8. (Optional) Cut up Cilantro and add to mash
